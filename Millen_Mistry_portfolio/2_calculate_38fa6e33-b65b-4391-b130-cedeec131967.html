Industry Glossary

RTP: "Return to Player." The % of all wagered money that a machine will pay back to players over time.

Hit Freq: How often a spin results in a win of any size. (33% means a win every 3 spins).

Volatility: The "Risk" level. High volatility means fewer wins but much larger jackpots.
